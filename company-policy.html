Company Policy

PT. BANDO INDONESIA, an industrial company that manufactures V-belts, Conveyor belts and Plastic parts with international quality levels is committing to :

IMPROVING Customer statisfaction in price quality, delivery, protecting and preserving the environment, and occupational health and safety (OHS), throught :

  • Fulfillment of Customers' Requirements, fulfillment of obligations and other requirements related to quality, environmental aspects and threats to OHS.
  • Develop a framework for the quality, environment, and OHS system in accordance with the objectives and context of the company.
  • Controlling Important Environmental Aspects to prevent pollution and protect the environment, control the use of natural resources and energy as well as control waste from product and service activities.
  • Provide safe and healthy working conditions to prevent occupational accidents and occupational diseases in accordance with the objectives, sizes and organizational contexts that are specifically defined based on OHS risks and opportunities and eliminate OHS hazards and risks.
  • Efficiency and continuous improvement of the effectiveness of the quality and environmental management system as well as Occupational Health and Safety.
  • Participation and consultation of workers in order to create an effective OHS management system.
