History

1987

PT. Bando Indonesia was established on November 25,1987 and began producing industrial and automotive power transmission belt.

1988

OEM ( Original Equipment manufacturing) automotive belts accepted and used as genuine parts by Japanese Automobile Manufacturer. Began export to Japan, Singapore.

1989

Began export to United States and Europe.

1995

PT. Bando Indonesia relocated production facility to new factory in 1995 and expanded its production to the conveyor belt business.

1996

Received the ISO 9002 certification from SGS in September 1996 for Power Transmission Belt.

1999

Automotive Belts accepted and used by American Car Manufacturer General Motor Indonesia. Began producing national brand V-Power brand to compete middle and cheap belt from domestic and import. Bando brand is target higher grade market.

2000

Received the QS 9000 certification from SGS in March 2000 for automotive belts. Received the ISO 9002 from SGS in September 2000 for conveyor belt.

2002

Received the ISO 14001 from SGS in March 2002. Relocated conveyor production to conveyor new factory.

2006

Received ISOTS 16949 from SGS in March 2006.

2010

Began producing variable speed belt for scooter bike.

2011

Acquire new land 2,1 hectars located infront of GT Complex.

2012

Build new warehouse at new land, relocated old warehouse to new warehouse, the old warehouse was used for production facility.

2011

Acquire new land 2,1 hectars located infront of GT Complex.

2012

Build new warehouse at new land, relocated old warehouse to new warehouse, the old warehouse was used for production facility.

2013

Build new conveyor factory at new land. Received the OHSAS 18001 for safety for safety from SGS in November 2013.

2014

Began producing plastic parts Weight Roller and slide pieces.

2016

Began producing steel cord conveyor belt.

2017

Began investment food grade sunline conveyor belt facility.

2018

Received ISO 45001 for safety and health from SGS.

2020

Add one biggest size 3-meter width conveyor belt production line.

Now and will continue to Grow.
